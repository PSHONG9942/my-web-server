📖 Scrabble Guide

🎯 Objective

Form valid English words on the board using your tiles to score the most points.

🕹️ How to Play

  • First Move: The first word must cover the center star (★).
  • Connection: Subsequent words must connect to existing tiles horizontally or vertically.
  • Validation: Words are strictly validated against the official Scrabble dictionary.
  • Blank Tiles: Blanks (?) act as any letter but are worth 0 points. You will be prompted to choose the letter when placing it.

🏆 Rules & Features

  • Multiplayer: The game supports up to 4 players.
  • Hints: Stuck? Use the 💡 Hint button! It costs 5 points (even if you drop below zero) and highlights the board and your rack tiles to suggest a move.
  • Bingo: Use all 7 tiles in one turn for a +50 point bonus!
  • Match Ending: The match ends if all active players pass their turn twice consecutively, or if a player finishes all their tiles when the bag is empty.
